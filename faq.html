Answers

1. What are your strengths in Tyler Perry Studios?
Good communication, positivity, and organization.
2. How do you measure success?
Being able to set realistic goals yet aggressive. Going the extra mile on all projects to satisfy clients.
3. What did you dislike about your old job?
"I did not like my manager or is not like the environment or I did not like the team". Keeping the focus on the positive not the negative.
4. What motivates you at the workplace?
The ability to achieve, recognition or challenging assignments.
5. How do you prioritize your work?
Depends on the situation. Labeling tsk with A, B, or C from most important to least.
6. What is your biggest fear?
Don't try to sugarcoat the answer by listing something ambitious as a fear, unless you truly mean it (for example: I fear being a great leader)
7. What is your perception of taking on risks?
Being able to take risks has to be calculated and improve the business with minimal risk.
8. Do you like being around people?
People skills are team skills. Enjoying interaction and working is a value in Tyler Perry Studios.
